FEATURED CASE STUDY

Single SMS Project & Cost Optimization

Cargill Enterprise Environment

Critical incident communications previously relied on traditional, high-overhead SMS text services. The costs associated with heavy SMS API usage across global geographies generated massive monthly operational expenses.

**The Solution & Impact**: Chetan led the initiative to incorporate the **Everbridge Mobile Application** into Cargill's primary core alert monitoring pipeline. By shifting real-time emergency responder routing and critical alerts from standard SMS to direct Everbridge App push notifications, the traditional SMS routing was completely bypassed. This successfully eliminated heavy SMS overhead costs, securing major financial savings and improving message delivery tracking.
